Welcome to After Hell! A thrilling 8-bit top-down shooter game where you battle through endless waves of enemies to survive.

## Features:
- 20 Different Maps
- 8 Different Weapons with Upgrades for Each
- Unique Background Music for each Level
- Retro Arcade-Like Graphics
- Wave-Based Survival
- Match Presets and Player Mods
- Keyboard and Mouse, and Controller Support
